"Cheers to Montana Made Beer!"

Our inspiration is this beautiful valley surrounded by mountains, streams and bountiful grain fields; the hardworking rural lifestyle of our community and our basset hounds Stanley and Leroy. They remind us every day of the importance of taking time to enjoy simple pleasures such as friendship, socializing, laughter and of course a pint or two (but not more than three!) of locally brewed craft beer.
